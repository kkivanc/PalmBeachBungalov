About Us
Palm Beach Bungalow

Our facility is located in the famous Ekincik Bay, known for its natural beauty, between Dalyan and Marmaris, just 50 km from Dalaman Airport. You can reach this unique natural paradise by traveling approximately 22 km towards the Döğüşbelen district after taking the Muğla-Marmaris-Fethiye road split on the 65th kilometer of the Muğla-Fethiye highway, before reaching Köyceğiz.

Our facility, which is right by the sea, offers a unique holiday atmosphere where blue and green meet. It is 22 km from the Kaunos Rock Tombs and 12 km from the healing Sultaniye Thermal Springs.

We invite you to this natural paradise where you can rejuvenate your body and soul with the unique beauty of the sea, sun, sand, and nature, while also enjoying a healthy diet and hygiene-focused vacation.

Let’s meet in this special atmosphere where quality and comfort merge, allowing you to escape the stress of daily life and embrace tranquility and peace.

In line with our commitment to your health and well-being, our bungalows and all common areas are regularly sterilized using the most advanced cleaning and disinfection methods. These procedures are meticulously repeated daily throughout your stay.

How about a peaceful holiday in the heart of nature, away from the crowds and noise, at Ekincik Bay, a hidden paradise where the Mediterranean and Aegean meet, with crystal-clear waters and a wide beach?

We are waiting for you to experience this unique getaway that will always remain in your memories and make you forget time.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What amenities are available in the bungalows?
    Our bungalows are equipped with air conditioning, a mini fridge, a fully equipped kitchen, a stove, a kettle, essential kitchen utensils, Wi-Fi, and a private veranda.
  • What are the check-in and check-out times?
    Check-in time is 14:00, and check-out time is 11:00. If you need an early check-in or late check-out, we can assist based on availability.
  • Is there a restaurant at the facility? Can we prepare our own meals?
    Yes, our facility has a restaurant and a beach bar. You can also prepare your own meals in the kitchen of your bungalow.
  • How far is the beach from the facility?
    The beach is located right in front of the facility, just a few steps away on foot.
  • Are sunbeds and umbrellas free of charge?
    Each guest staying in our facility is provided with 2 sun loungers and 1 umbrella free of charge.
  • Is the facility child-friendly?
    Absolutely! With our spacious green areas, safe play spaces for children, and family-friendly environment, our facility is ideal for families with children.
  • Do you accept pets?
    Unfortunately, we do not accept pets in our facility.
  • Is there a parking area at the facility?
    Yes, we provide a free parking area exclusively for our guests.
  • What activities are available at the facility?
    Our facility offers beach volleyball, canoeing, trekking, bicycle rentals, a barbecue area, and various nature hiking trails.
  • Are electricity, water, and Wi-Fi included in the accommodation price?
    Yes, these services are included in the accommodation price, and no additional fee is charged.
  • Is there a refund policy for reservation cancellations?
    Reservation cancellations and changes are subject to our facility's cancellation policy. For details, please contact us.
  • Is smoking allowed in the bungalows?
    Smoking is prohibited indoors. However, you can smoke on the veranda and in outdoor areas.
